📖How the Fraud Unfolded

On 1 March 2025, Ramesh received a WhatsApp invite to “Mumbai Elite Traders” – a group with 248 fake members. A “stock expert” posted daily profit screenshots. Two days later, “Priya Sharma” started a private conversation, building trust over family and markets. She claimed to have made ₹2.3 lakhs using an invite‑only platform called TradePro India.

🔑 Social Engineering: The group fabricated legitimacy with SEBI branding and bot testimonials.

On 5 March, Priya sent an APK link (not from Play Store) and a referral code. Ramesh installed the app, which looked professional. His first ₹5,000 investment showed a fake profit of ₹8,400 – and he was allowed to withdraw it. That hooked him.

⚠️ Classic “pig butchering” – fatten the victim with a small win before the slaughter.

Over the next days, Ramesh invested ₹75,000, then ₹3,00,000. When he tried to withdraw, he was told to pay 18% GST (₹2,00,000) and later an RBI audit fee (₹1,65,000). After paying, all contacts went dark. Total loss: ₹8,40,000.

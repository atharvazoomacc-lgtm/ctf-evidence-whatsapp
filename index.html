📖How The Crime Happened — Full Narrative

1 March 2025 — The Hook
Ramesh receives a WhatsApp group invite from an unknown number. The group is named "Mumbai Elite Traders 🚀 | SEBI Tips" with 248 fake members. A "stock expert" called Dr. Arvind Kapoor posts daily "free tips" with screenshots of huge gains.

🔑 Social Engineering Tactic: The group creates a false sense of legitimacy using SEBI branding and fake testimonials.

2 March 2025 — Trust Building
A profile named "Priya Sharma" messages Ramesh privately. She claims to be a financial analyst. Over 3 days she builds rapport — discussing family, work, markets. She shares that she made ₹2.3 lakhs using a "premium trading platform" called TradePro India.

🔑 Technique — Pig Butchering: Scammers invest time building deep trust before asking for money.

5 March 2025 — The Bait (App Download)
Priya sends a link to download the TradePro India APK directly (not from Play Store). A fake "Relationship Manager" calls from an Indian VoIP number. The app looks professional with live stock prices and a portfolio dashboard.

8 March 2025 — First Small Win
Ramesh invests ₹5,000. Within 2 days the app shows a profit of ₹8,400. He withdraws successfully – scammers allow the first withdrawal to build confidence.

⚠️ Classic Trick: Scammers always allow the first small withdrawal. This "proof of concept" hooks the victim completely.

10–13 March 2025 — Escalating Investments
Ramesh invests ₹75,000 on March 10. The app shows ₹1,42,000 within 3 days. On March 13, he transfers ₹3,00,000. The app now shows his portfolio at ₹5,28,000.

15 March 2025 — The Squeeze Begins (Fake Taxes)
When Ramesh tries to withdraw, the app shows an error: "You must pay 18% GST on profits as per SEBI mandate." He transfers ₹2,00,000 to a "GST collection account".

17 March 2025 — RBI Audit Scam
A fake "RBI representative" calls from a spoofed number and demands a ₹1,65,000 "audit clearance fee". Ramesh pays. Total loss: ₹8,40,000.

20 March 2025 — Vanishing Act
All WhatsApp contacts go dark. The TradePro India app shows "Server Maintenance." The website goes offline.
